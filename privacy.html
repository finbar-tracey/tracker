Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

1. Introduction

Gym Tracker ("the App") is a personal fitness tracking application designed for individual use. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

2. Data Collection

The App collects and stores the following types of data:

  • Workout data (exercises, sets, reps, weights)
  • Health metrics (sleep duration, heart rate, HRV, stress levels)
  • Nutrition data (calories, macronutrients)
  • Body metrics (weight, body fat percentage)
  • Progress photos (optional)
  • Data synced from connected wearable devices (Garmin, Whoop, Apple Watch)

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using browser local storage. No data is transmitted to external servers except when:

  • Syncing with Garmin Connect API to retrieve your health and fitness data
  • Syncing with Whoop API to retrieve your recovery and performance data
  • Syncing with Apple Health (via local device access only)

4. Data Usage

Your data is used solely for:

  • Displaying your fitness progress and health metrics
  • Calculating readiness scores and workout recommendations
  • Tracking personal records and achievements
  • Analyzing trends and correlations in your health and performance

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties. Your health and fitness information remains private and is only accessible by you on your device.

6. Third-Party APIs

The App integrates with the following third-party services to retrieve your data:

  • Garmin Connect API: To sync sleep, heart rate, stress, Body Battery, and activity data from your Garmin devices
  • Whoop API: To sync recovery scores, strain scores, sleep performance, and HRV data from your Whoop device
  • Apple Health: To access health data stored on your iPhone (with your permission)

These integrations only retrieve data you have already collected through these devices. We do not share any additional information with these services.

7. Data Security

Your data is protected through:

  • Local storage on your device (not on external servers)
  • Secure OAuth authentication for API connections
  • HTTPS encryption for all API communications
  • API credentials stored securely in serverless functions

8. Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device until you choose to delete it. You can clear all data at any time through the Settings menu in the App.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your data at any time
  • Export your data in JSON format
  • Delete all your data permanently
  • Disconnect any wearable device integration
  • Revoke API access at any time

10. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

12. Contact

This is a personal project for individual use. If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact the developer through the GitHub repository.

13. Scope

Important: This App is designed for personal, non-commercial use by a single individual. It is not a commercial product or service.
